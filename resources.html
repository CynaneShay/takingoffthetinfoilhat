EXvangelical Resources

Reading Recommendations

When I "deconstructed" from White Christian Nationalism, I did not have the language to describe it. I did not know there were resources or others like me. I needed information. I needed direction to rebuild a worldview from the ground up. I had to reapproach history, theology, politics, psychology, and philosophy. I had to reframe evangelical issues like Hell, feminism, LGBTQ+ affirmation, and purity culture. I felt like I needed to dig into what had been a lie in order to build a new, healthier understanding of the world.

These are the tools I found. They helped me immensely, and I sincerely hope they can help others find answers and find peace.
