EXvangelical Resources

When I "deconstructed" from White Christian Nationalism, I did not have the language to describe it. I did not know there were resources or others like me. I needed information. I had to reapproach history, theology, politics, psychology, and philosophy. I had to reframe evangelical issues like Hell, feminism, LGBTQ+ affirmation, and purity culture. I needed to dig out what had been a lie and rebuild an entire worldview from the ground up.

These are the tools I found. They helped me immensely, and I hope they can help you.
